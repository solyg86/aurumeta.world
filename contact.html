Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aurumeta.World?

‚ñº

Aurumeta.World is a revolutionary real estate investment platform that democratizes property ownership through blockchain tokenization. We combine ancient Stoic wisdom with Pop-Art accessibility to make premium real estate investments available to everyone.

How does real estate tokenization work?

‚ñº

We transform physical real estate properties into digital tokens on the blockchain. Each token represents a fractional ownership share in the property, allowing you to invest with smaller amounts while maintaining true ownership rights and receiving proportional rental income.

What is PopStoicism?

‚ñº

PopStoicism is our unique philosophy that combines Marcus Aurelius' Stoic principles of wisdom, virtue, and long-term thinking with Andy Warhol's Pop-Art approach to making complex concepts accessible and beautiful for everyone.
