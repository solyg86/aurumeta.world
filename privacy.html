Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to our PopStoic philosophy. We believe in transparency, respect, and ethical data practices that align with our community values.

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Aurumeta.World, we operate under the PopStoic principle that true strength comes from virtue and transparency. This Privacy Policy reflects our commitment to protecting your personal information while building a revolutionary real estate investment platform.

PopStoic Privacy Principle: We collect only what we need, protect what we collect, and respect your rights at every step. Your trust is the foundation of our community.

This policy applies to all users of Aurumeta.World, including our website, mobile applications, and all related services. By using our platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information that enables us to provide secure, compliant, and personalized real estate investment services.

Personal Information

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Email address and phone number
  • Residential address
  • Government-issued ID (KYC compliance)
  • Financial information for verification

Investment Data

  • Portfolio holdings and transactions
  • Investment preferences and risk profile
  • Property interests and voting history
  • DAO participation and governance activities

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage patterns and platform interactions
  • Wallet addresses and blockchain transactions

Communication Data

  • Support inquiries and correspondence
  • Community forum posts and comments
  • Feedback and survey responses
  • Marketing communication preferences

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Platform Services: Process investments, manage portfolios, and facilitate property ownership
  • Compliance: Meet KYC/AML requirements and regulatory obligations
  • Security: Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and maintain platform integrity
  • Governance: Enable DAO participation and community decision-making
  • Communication: Send important updates, notifications, and requested information

Secondary Uses (With Your Consent)

  • Personalization: Customize investment recommendations and user experience
  • Analytics: Improve platform performance and develop new features
  • Marketing: Share relevant opportunities and community updates
  • Research: Conduct studies to improve real estate tokenization
PopStoic Commitment: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to serve you and our community better.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share your information only when necessary and with appropriate protections:

Service Providers

We work with trusted partners who help us operate our platform:

  • KYC/AML Providers: Identity verification and compliance screening
  • Payment Processors: Secure transaction processing
  • Cloud Services: Data storage and platform hosting
  • Analytics Partners: Platform performance and user experience improvement

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
  • Investigate potential violations of our terms
  • Respond to verified requests from law enforcement

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction, with continued protection under this policy.

Data Security & Protection

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption standards.

Access Control

Multi-factor authentication and role-based access to limit data exposure.

Infrastructure

Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits and monitoring.

Staff Training

Regular privacy and security training for all team members.

Blockchain Security: Property ownership records are secured on the blockchain, providing immutable and transparent ownership verification while maintaining privacy through pseudonymous addresses.

Access
Request copies of your personal data
Rectification
Correct inaccurate information
Erasure
Request deletion of your data

Export your data in standard formats
Restriction

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below. We will respond within 30 days and may request identity verification for security purposes.

Note: Some rights may be limited by legal obligations (e.g., KYC requirements) or legitimate business interests (e.g., fraud prevention).

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and improve our platform:

Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant content and track campaign effectiveness
Cookie Control: You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings or through our cookie consent manager. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for 7 years after closure for compliance purposes
  • Transaction Records: Maintained for 10 years to meet financial and tax obligations
  • Communication Data: Kept for 3 years unless longer retention is required for legal purposes
  • Marketing Data: Deleted immediately upon unsubscribe or after 2 years of inactivity

Blockchain records are permanent by design, but personal identifiers are pseudonymized to protect privacy.

Children's Privacy

Aurumeta.World is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

As a global platform, we may transfer your personal data to countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with adequate data protection laws
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved contracts for data protection
  • Certification Schemes: Privacy Shield and similar frameworks where applicable
  • Binding Corporate Rules: Internal policies ensuring consistent protection
EU Users: We comply with GDPR requirements for international transfers and provide the same level of protection regardless of where your data is processed.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • We will notify you via email or platform notification
  • We will post the updated policy on our website
  • We will update the "Last Modified" date
  • For material changes, we may require your explicit consent

We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
